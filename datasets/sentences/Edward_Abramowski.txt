abramowski born stefanin today's ukraine jadwiga edward
after mother died 1878 moved warsaw 1879 teacher maria konopnicka introduced members first proletariat
influenced leo tolstoy abramowski called himself state-rejecting socialist most important work socialism & state
went further political philosophy other works such republic friends general collusion against government
later years thought increasingly tended towards anarcho-syndicalism emphasising importance co-operative organization work force
abramowski considered founder polish co-operative movement promoting economic associations & initiatives
cooperativist founded biggest countrywide consumer cooperative spolem together cofounded polish socialist party
alongside politico-social theorising also conducted intense research activity field experimental psychology showing particular interest subconscious
gave certain notoriety abroad & 1916 given chair experimental psychology university warsaw occupied until death
