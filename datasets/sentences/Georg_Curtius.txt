georg curtius georg curtius april 16 1820 august 12 1885 german philologist
biography curtius born lübeck brother historian archeologist ernst curtius
after education bonn berlin three years schoolmaster dresden until 1845 returned berlin university privatdocent
1849 placed charge philological seminary prague two years later appointed professor classical philology prague university
1852 moved prague similar appointment kiel again 1862 kiel leipzig
philological theories exercised widespread influence
important publications die sprachvergleichung ihrem verhältniss zur classischen philologie 1845 eng
fh trithen 1851 sprachvergleichende beiträge zur griechischen und lateinischen grammatik 1846 grundzüge griechischen etymologie 1858-1862 5 th ed
1879 das verbum griechischen sprache 1873
last two works translated english augustus samuel wilkins edwin bourdieu england
1878 till death curtius general editor leipziger studien zur classischen philologie
griechische schulgrammatik first published 1852 passed through twenty editions edited english
last work zur kritik neuesten sprachforschung 1885 attacks views emerging neogrammarian school philology
curtius died hermsdorf am kynast aged 65 succeeded leipzig karl brugmann
