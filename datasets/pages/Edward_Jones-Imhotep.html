Edward Jones-Imhotep is a historian of technology, academic and currently an associate professor at York University.[1] He received a B.A. from York University in 1995 and a Ph.D. from Harvard in 2001.[2][3] He was a recipient of the Mellon Fellowship from the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation in Humanistic Studies in 1995.

Imhotep's research lies at the intersection of historical and philosophical questions surrounding the modern physical sciences and technology. His work has been published in many scientific journals in the United States, Canada and all over the world.
