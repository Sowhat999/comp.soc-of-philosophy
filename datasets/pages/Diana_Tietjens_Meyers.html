Diana Meyers is a philosopher working in the philosophy of action and in the philosophy of feminism. Meyers is Professor Emerita of Philosophy at the University of Connecticut.

Biography[edit]

Diana Meyers holds a Bachelor's degree from the University of Chicago and a Master's degree and PhD from The Graduate Center, CUNY.[1] She mostly works "in three main areas of philosophy: philosophy of action, feminist ethics, and human rights theory.[2]

Distinguished Woman Philosopher Award[edit]

In December 2012, Diana Meyers was awarded the Distinguished Woman Philosopher award at the 2012 APA Eastern Conference.
