Kevin Mulligan (born 23 June 1951) is a British philosopher, working on ontology, the philosophy of mind, and the philosophy of logic. He is currently Professor for philosophy at the University of Geneva, where he collaborates with, among others, Pascal Engel. He is also known for his work with Peter Simons and Barry Smith on Austrian and Polish philosophy.
