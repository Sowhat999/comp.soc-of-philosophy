Sediq Afghan (Pashto/Dari: صدیق افغان , Sediq Afġān) is an Afghan Philosopher and mathematician. He is the founder and head of the "World philosophical Math Research Center" in Kabul, Afghanistan. He is also a political activist. He had a prominent role in protests in Kabul about Afghanistan- and Islam-related issues, including an anti-American protest in 2003,[1] a hunger strike to protest over beatings of journalists by Afghan security officers in 2006, and another one to protest the 2008 Danish Muhammad cartoons.[2]
