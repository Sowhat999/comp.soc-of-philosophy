Heinrich Christoph Wilhelm von Sigwart (31 August 1789 – 16 November 1844) was a German philosopher and logician. He was the father of Christoph von Sigwart (28 March 1830 – 4 August 1904 ), who also was a philosopher and logician.

Life[edit]

Sigwart was born into a family with a long history of philosophers, theologians and physicians at Remmingsheim in Württemberg. He became professor of philosophy at Tübingen, and wrote numerous books on the history of philosophy. He died in Stuttgart.
