Trenton Merricks (/ˈmɛriks/) is a philosopher[1] at the University of Virginia. His main fields are metaphysics, epistemology, and philosophy of religion.[2]

Works[edit]

  • Objects and Persons (Oxford University Press, 2001)
  • Truth and Ontology (Oxford University Press, 2007)
