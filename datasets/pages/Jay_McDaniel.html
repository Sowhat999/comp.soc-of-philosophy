Jay B. McDaniel is an American philosopher and theologian. McDaniel is a Professor of Religion at Hendrix College, where he is the Director of the Steel Center for the Study of Religion and Philosophy, and specializes in Buddhism, Whiteheadian process philosophy and process theology, and ecological theology.[1]

In addition to publishing numerous books and articles on theology, McDaniel is one of the most active voices for process thought on social media. He runs the website jesusjazzbuddhism.org,[2] and created a series of videos entitled "What is Process Thought?"[3]
