Marco Tarchi (born October 11, 1952 in Rome[1]) is an Italian political scientist. He is currently full professor of Political Science, Political Theory and Political Communication at the Cesare Alfieri School of Political Sciences of the University of Florence.[2] His research is focused primarily on populism, democracy, political organization, and extreme right.

He obtained his Ph.D. in Political Science at the University of Florence in 1987.[2] In the same university, he was assistant professor with tenure in Political Science from 1993 to 1998 and then associate professor from 1998 to 2001.[2] He is full professor since 2001.[2] He was also visiting professor at the universities of Turku (1993, 1996-1998, 2003, 2007), Santiago and Viña del Mar (2004), and del Rosario (2008).[2]
