Sufi cosmology (Arabic: الكوزمولوجية الصوفية‎) is a general term for cosmological doctrines associated with Sufism. It is a Sufi approach which discusses the creation of man and the universe, which according to mystics are the fundamental grounds upon which Islamic religious universe is based. According to Sufi cosmology, God's reason for the creation of this universe and mankind is the "manifestation" and "recognition" of Himself as it is stated in Hadith Qudsi – "I was a hidden Treasure; I desired to be recognized so I created the creature".[1]

Emanation[edit]

Islamic sufis describe the Divine Descent and the creation of universe and mankind in the following stages, when Noor-e-Ahadi (Light of One), coming out of His self-isolated oneness, intended to manifest Himself in multiplicity. These stages are also termed as “Tanzalat-e-Satta.[2] Many saints have explained these Tanzalat-e-Satta in their books. Abu Saeed Mubarak Makhzoomi has discussed them in his Arabic book called Tohfa Mursala.[3] They are also discussed in the exegesis of Fusoos-ul-Hikam, a book by Ibn Arabi.[4]

  • Alam-i-HaHoot (Realm of He-ness): The level of HaHoot refers to HaHooiyat(The Unknowable and Incomparable world). It is an Arabic term which pertains to the Divine's Essence prior to manifestation. The spiritual stage related to it is called Ahdiyat(Alonehood). This is the Realm of pre-existence and a level of non creation.[5] According to sufis, this state of Ahdiyat is incapable of being conceived, incapable of interpretation and is beyond all logical bounds. At this stage Allah is all alone and hidden in his own veil and cannot be exemplified with anything. Nothing exists here, neither the universe nor the creation. Here the Zaat (Divine essence) and neither His attributes are revealed or manifested.[6] He is called 'Hoo' here instead of Allah by mystics or sufis instead of Allah. Because Allah is the One to be worshiped but He cannot be worshiped unless there is a worshipper.[7]
  • Alam-i-Yahoot (Realm of First Manifestation): The spiritual stage pertaining to YaHoot is referred to as Wahdat (Oneness). YaHoot is the first level of manifestation of "Hoo". When the Divine Essence desired to manifest, He manifested Himself in the form of Noor-e-Mohammad (Light of Mohammad — which Allah swt created from the reflection of His own light.). The first creation was Noor-e-Muhammad and it is also said that the Pen was created first; when Allah swt said “Qun” i.e “Be” and then it was for the first time; if one look at the letters of “Kaaf” and “Nun” of the word “Qun”, then it is accordance with the fact that Noor-e-Muhammadi saw was the first creation; this is because letter “Kaaf” was the indication of Allah’s kalaam i.e Allah’s statement, that He swt is going to create something. And the authority Imam Suyuti — whom everybody take as a source of interpreting Qur’an and aHadith — say that the “Nun” is “the Pen” and “the Pen” is the “Nun” — and the holy family member, Imam Jafar-as-Sadiq ra. from the fourth caliph Ali ra narrated that “Nun” is “Najm” i.e star — and we have Quranverse:...wan najme iza sajaa... — and “Najm” is “wujud e Muhammad saw” i.e the creation of haqeeqat-e-Muhammad saw. So as we see: “Nun” is both “Pen” and “the first creation of muhammad saw” — which is unknown to everthing else — which form it had and how it looked like — besides the One who created him and the one who was being created i.e Allah swt and His Prophet saw. So before “Qun” that unknown creation was hidden, and after the command “Qun” was implied then it became visible in the form of the unknown of Haqeeqat-e-Muhammadi saw. And what is word in between this two case i.e hidden to visible, the word “Qun” i located and this word has been explained above. So after that the first creation right after the command “Qun” the unknown form of Holy Prophet Muhammad saw was created. And where it that form’s reflected, those sites became “Akhira” and the places which did receive its shade, became Paradise, and the place which did not receive its shade, became Hell i.e Jahannum. Then it became visible for a second time — in the form of noor — and where it reflected upon, those area became Alam-e-Arwah — souls of prophets, and angels where created and they so this noor. Then it became visible for the last time i.e the third time and that was in the holy womb of Amina ra., mother of Prophet Muhammad saw — after the noor had been traveling thorough all the pure souls from Prophet Adam through all holy human beings — and its shade was poured over the physical universe, and that is the reason why one can't find the shade of Holy Prophet saw shade in this earth, and all physical planets. Holy Prophet’s saw shade is the paradise. In short: Haqeeqat-e-Muhammadi saw and Nooraniyat-e-Muhammadi saw and last Bashriyat (human form but not like any human)-e-Muhammadi saw. The form of the first one is unknown to anyone except Allah swt and His Holy Prophet saw — and this was the first creation of Holy Prophet Muhammad saw — right after the command was set “Qun”. So this human mind is unable to grasp the status and to know the form of that Haqeeqat-e-Muhammadi saw. Holy Prophet saw is nur and also bashar and he saw is also not nur and not bashar i.e if one reflects upon the first creation i.e Haqeeqat e Muhammadi saw — the high status of Holy Prophet saw cannot be grasped by this earth who was’nt even created when the Holy Prophet saw was created right after Allah swt for the first time said “Qun”. [8] Since YaHoot is a world of Oneness, so it clarifies that Noor of Mohammad is a manifestation of God’s Essence and is not a separate entity. This is the first step towards Divine descent. The creation has not started yet. Here Hoo is called Allah which in Arabic means "the worshippable one", because there is a being in the form of Noor of Mohammad who worships Allah. And the level is called yaHoot because there is an entity who would address Hoo as YaHoo (O’Hoo).
  • Alam-i-LaHoot (Realm of Absolute Unity): The world of LaHoot is related to the level of Wahdiyat(Unity). Here the soul of Mohammad is manifested from the Noor of Mohammad. Since this is a world of Unity so at this level Noor of Allah and Noor of Mohammad are in the same form. Therefore the soul of Mohammad is infact the soul of Allah called Rooh-e-Qudsi(The Divine Soul). About this Divine soul, Allah states in Quran in Surah Hijar “And then I breathed my Soul into Him”.[9] At this level the whole universe and its creation is hidden in Noor-e-Mohammad and is anxious to manifest itself.

The above three levels are the levels of Unity and non-creation. The presence of Hoo in HaHoot, YaHoot and LaHoot indicates Absolute Unity. The creation takes place in the coming levels of Jabrut, Malakut and Nasut.

  • Alam-i-Jabrut (Realm of Power):Jabrut is an Arabic term which means 'bridge' or anything that connects two things. It is called Jabrut because it connects the above three levels of non-creation and the coming levels of creation. The level of Jabrut is related to the level of creation of souls of human beings. Here Allah created the souls of human beings from Rooh-e-Qudsi. The soul of human beings created from Rooh-e-Qudsi is called Rooh-e-Sultani(the Kingly soul) .The Divine attributes like knowledge, wisdom etc which were present in totality in Rooh-e-Qudsi at the above non-creation levels got divided into human beings.[10] Rooh-e-Sultani has attributes of angels because angels were created at the same level. Here Rooh-e-Qudsi got concealed in the Rooh-e-Sultani.
  • Alam-e-Malakut (Realm of Intelligence): This level refers to the world of symbolic forms. At this level the souls took a recognizable but immaterial and intangible form for the first time. Here Rooh-e-sultani got concealed in the cover of Rooh-Noorani(the soul of celestial light). The souls of animals, plants and all non-living things were also created at this level.Due to the creation of souls of animals at this level the human soul adopted beastal qualities like lust,greed, covet etc.
  • Alam-i-Nasut (Realm of Physical bodies): The level of Nasut refers to the physical world of material bodies. The stage where foundations of the tangible world of matter are laid. Here Rooh-e-Noorani got concealed in the cover of Rooh-e-Hewani (the soul directly related to the physical body) which was the breathed into the physical bodies of flesh and bones and non-living things. The process of human creation got completed here and Allah manifested Himself completely in Humans by concealing in the covers of Rooh-e-Qudsi, Rooh-e-Sultani, Rooh-e-Noorani and Rooh-e-Hewani.
