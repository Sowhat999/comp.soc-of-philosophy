John Steel in 2004

John Robert Steel (born October 30, 1948) is an American set theorist at University of California, Berkeley (formerly at UCLA). He has made many contributions to the theory of inner models and determinacy. With Donald A. Martin, he proved projective determinacy, assuming the existence of sufficient large cardinals. He earned his Ph.D. in Logic & the Methodology of Science at Berkeley in 1977.

In 2015 the European Set Theory Society awarded him and Ronald Jensen the Hausdorff medal for their paper "K without a measurable".
