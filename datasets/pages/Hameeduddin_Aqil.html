Hameeduddin Husami Husammi, Urdu: حميد الدين‎, (July 23, 1928 – March 12, 2010) was an Indian Muslim religious scholar. Born in Hyderabad Deccan, (today's Hyderabad) in India, the second son of Fazil Husami.
