Christer Sturmark

Nils Gösta Christer Sturmark, born 7 September 1964 in Danderyd, Stockholm County, is a Swedish author, IT-entrepreneur and prominent debater on religion and humanism in Swedish media.

He holds a B.A. in computer science and owns several small companies in the IT sector. He is an atheist[1] and chairman of the Swedish Humanist Association, Humanisterna.
