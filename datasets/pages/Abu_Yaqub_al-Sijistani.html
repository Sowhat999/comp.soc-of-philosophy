Not to be confused with Abu Sulayman al-Sijistani.

Abu Yaqub al-Sijistani (Persian: ابو یعقوب سجستانی‎‎) (active 971 CE) was an Persian Ismaili missionary and Neo-Platonic philosopher, who died sometime around 971 CE[1]
