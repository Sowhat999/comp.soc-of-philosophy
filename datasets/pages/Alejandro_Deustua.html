Alejandro Octavio Deustua Escarza (March 22, 1849 in Huancayo – August 6, 1945 in Lima) was a teacher, a creative researcher, a humanist, a philosopher. Dr. Deustua studied in Guadalupe National School and he graduated from Lima’s Universidad de San Marcos.

His parents were Remigio Deustua and Toribia Escarza.
