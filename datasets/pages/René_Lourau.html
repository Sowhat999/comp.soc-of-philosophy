René Lourau (1933, Gelos – 11 January 2000) was a French sociologist and educator.

Works in Castilian[edit]

  • The State and the Unconscious. Barcelona: Kairos. 1980. ISBN 847-245-116-X.
  • The institutional analysis. Buenos Aires: Routledge. 1991. ISBN 950-518-052-7.
  • Freedom of movement. An Introduction to Institutional Analysis. Buenos Aires: Eudeba . 2000. ISBN 950-23-1140-X.
  • Intellectuals and power. Montevideo: Nordan Community. 2001. ISBN 9974-42-079-2.
  • The unconscious state. Buenos Aires: Terramar. 2009. ISBN 978-987-617-064-2.
