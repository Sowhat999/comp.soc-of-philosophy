Sean Dorrance Kelly is an American philosopher and Professor and Chair of the Department of Philosophy at Harvard University. He is known for his expertise on various aspects of the philosophical, phenomenological, and cognitive neuroscientific nature of human experience.[1][2] He is featured in Tao Ruspoli's film Being in the World.
